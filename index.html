Nice to meet you, I’m Mahdi

I am a passionate and results-driven software developer with a strong focus on web development, especially within the **MERN stack** (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js). I have developed a deep understanding of creating modern, scalable web applications and strive to always deliver innovative solutions. Over the years, I’ve worked on a variety of projects, ranging from dynamic, data-driven web applications to mobile app development, honing my skills with the latest technologies.

Currently, I am proud to be part of **Zetabox** as a **Flutter Developer**, where I am actively contributing to the development of cutting-edge, cross-platform mobile applications. This role has given me the opportunity to broaden my expertise in mobile development and has allowed me to deepen my knowledge in **Flutter**, enabling me to create highly efficient, user-centric applications that perform seamlessly on both Android and iOS.

In addition to my role at Zetabox, I am also the founder and lead developer of **Takwira**, a football reservation platform designed to simplify the process of organizing and booking football games. I built both the front-end and back-end of the platform using **React.js**, **Node.js**, and **MongoDB**, focusing on key features such as user authentication, real-time messaging, and intuitive scheduling tools for field owners. Taqwira is a testament to my ability to create innovative solutions and manage full-stack web applications from concept to deployment.

Previously, I had the privilege of working at **Primatec Engineering** as an **Automotive Software Developer** (internship), where I gained invaluable experience in embedded systems and automotive software development. I worked on the development of high-performance applications and contributed to projects that required deep technical knowledge of both hardware and software integration. This experience taught me the importance of precision and performance in the development of embedded systems, and I continue to apply these principles to my work today.

As a **Full Stack Instructor** at **GOMYCODE**, I have the privilege of teaching the **MERN stack** and **Python** to aspiring developers. My approach to teaching is rooted in the philosophy of "learning by doing," which I believe is essential for mastering the craft of programming. Through hands-on projects, I ensure my students acquire practical, real-world skills, preparing them for successful careers in the tech industry. It is incredibly rewarding to see my students grow and become confident developers capable of tackling complex challenges.

Outside of my professional life, I am passionate about football, which I believe helps fuel my creativity and problem-solving skills. I also hold a **Bachelor's degree in Data Analysis and Big Data**, and I am currently pursuing a degree in **Web and Internet Technologies**. I am always looking to learn new things, expand my skillset, and make meaningful contributions to the tech community. I am committed to continuous learning and growth, ensuring that I stay at the forefront of the ever-evolving world of software development.

Feel free to reach out — I’m always happy to connect and collaborate!
